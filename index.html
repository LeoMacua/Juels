Artist Bio

Julliette Kimathi AKA JUELS✨ is a Kenyan female singer/songwriter/rapper. She is a versatile artist who’s sound is a soulful blend of RnB, Reggae, DanceHall & Afropop. Her singer and song writing skills had her picked as one of the songwriters for music in a series by Foxton Media Ltd, amongst them are songs like ‘Mpakani Tuko Ready’, ‘We Be Happening’ and in the same year co wrote a Peace song called ‘Rise’ CEDO ft Machakos Boys which bagged 2nd place in a UN Peace Award - United Nations Disarmament Affairs & Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Republic of Korea. She is well known for her soulful performances and has perfected her craft or story telling and wordplay through her music. She has performed in restaurants across the city with her band The Krafted, The GogoSimo Band, has been featured on KIKE concert tour, GOLD Nairobi to name a few. She has also done background vocals for a few artists such as Thomas Hype, Muringi, Steph Kapela, H_art the Band and CEDO. She is on a mission to inspire and invoke change through her music. She released her debut single ‘ FOR ME’ in April 2023, which you can find on all streaming platforms, including her latest singles.
